Help

Course Site Generator is a desktop Java application for creating and editing course sites. The app presents five tabs: Site, Syllabus, Meeting Times, Office Hours, and Schedule. Site edits the basic information and layout of the course site. Syllabus tab edits the speicfic informations about the course. Meeting Time tab edits the time and location where students meet every day. Office Hour tab edits the instructors' office hours and locations. The Schedule tab creates a calendar on all speical events during the semester. The application saves data to a JSON format that can be used by Web pages for displaying the course site.

Step 1: Edit Site

To start, enter basic information of the course and the instructor.

Step 2: Edit Syllabus

Enter the syllabus of the course.

Step 3: Edit Meeting Time

Enter Lecture, Recitation, and Lab details for the course.

Step 4: Edit Office Hours

Enter Office Hours of the TAs.

Step 5: Edit Schedule

Enter Holidays, HW due todays, and lecture days and topics for the course.

Save your work

Once you've completed your work, you can press the save button and give your file a name. Note that the name in the app text field will be the name used in the Recent Work list of the Welcome dialog.
